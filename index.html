Our Mission

Here at Civitas, our mission is to connect and unite our community. We believe everyone should have access to the resources they need so that we can grow together. Explore our collection of resources to help support your current situations whether it's homelessness, poverty and domestic violence. We are here to help and support you through your needs. Every resource here has been collected from sites that serve the Mountlake Terrace area and neighboring cities, to ensure that our resources are local and appplicable to you. If you have ideas of a resource you would like to see added, feel free to submit a form or search it's name in our search section. Dive in and discover what we have to share.
